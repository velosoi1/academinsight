About AcademInsight - Biber Tagger

AcademInsight (Biber Tagger version) classifies your text for discipline and communicative purpose drawing on the results of a Machine Learning model. This ML model was built based on the results of previous research that sought to describe the lexico-grammatical characteristics of undergraduate student writing (Gardner, Nesi and Biber, 2018; Hardy and Romer, 2013; Larsson et al, 2021, Goulart, 2021; in press). That is, Gardner, Nesi and Biber (2018), for example, showed that verb complement clauses and subordinate clauses were more common in Humanities, while premodifying nouns, attributive adjectives, and prepositions were more common in Physical Sciences.
